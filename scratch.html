About This Page

Welcome! This page explores the routines, goals, and activities that help me stay balanced and motivated as a student. It includes lists, an image, an embedded video, an on-page anchor, and a live Tableau graph — all built from scratch without any Bootstrap.

My Weekly Priorities

  1. Healthy habits
    • Drink at least 8 glasses of water
    • Go on morning or evening walks
    • Get 7–8 hours of sleep
    • Eat something nutritious for breakfast
  2. School goals
    • Stay on top of readings and assignments
    • Attend office hours when needed
    • Review notes within 24 hours of class
  3. Time with family and friends
  4. Personal growth and reflection
